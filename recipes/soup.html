Soup Recipe

Description

This soup recipe features a hearty blend of vegetables, beans, and spices simmered to perfection. Ideal for a comforting meal on a chilly day!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add diced carrots, celery, and bell pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes until vegetables start to soften.
  3. Add diced tomatoes (with juice), kidney beans, vegetable broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add frozen corn and cook for an additional 5 minutes until heated through.
  6. Adjust seasoning if necessary. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Enjoy your warm and comforting soup!
