Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This spaghetti recipe features al dente pasta tossed in a rich tomato sauce with garlic, herbs, and a hint of chili. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and let simmer for 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy your delicious spaghetti!
